Annotations (1)

  • rdfs:comment "An ethical issue arises when an action causes an impact on certain entities, that are capable of receiving the consequences, while violating some ethical principles. An extension of this is an ethical dilemma, in which more than one courses of actions lead to certain ethical issues, making it difficult to choose between the available actions."
