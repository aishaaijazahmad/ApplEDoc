Annotations (1)

  • rdfs:comment "An ethical principle is a concept that describes some virtue that must be upheld to preseve the morality of an action.

    For example, autonomy is an ethical principle which must be upheld so as to not violate some basic aspect of human rights. The violation of this would be unethical."
