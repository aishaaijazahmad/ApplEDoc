Annotations (1)

  • rdfs:comment "A domain adheres to a specific type of applied ethics. For example, the medical domain adheres to bioethics. Again, this connection is not absolute. For example, an issue of the medical domain may also require adherance to the professional domain, when considering the treatment of nurses and doctors in large private hospitals."
